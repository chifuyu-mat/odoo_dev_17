Odoo Hotel Management Solution

"Absolute Hotel Management In Odoo" Odoo Hotel Management Solution allows you to create a hotel booking website within the Odoo system effortlessly. It provides a wide range of hotel management features designed to streamline the booking process, ensuring it is smooth and efficient for both hotel owners and guests.

Frontend View for Hotel Display Easily showcase hotels on your website with both grid and list view options. Each hotel has its own detailed page featuring images, description, services, policies, reviews, and an interactive map for location. This setup helps customers quickly explore hotel options and room availability for a better booking experience.
Owner & User Dashboards The Odoo Hotel Management System offers dedicated dashboards for both owners and users. Owners can easily track bookings, revenue, and performance through real-time data, while users can manage their bookings and view stay history. The clean and simple layout ensures a smooth and informed experience for both.
Manage Multiple Properties Easily manage multiple hotels from a single backend. View, edit, and update each hotel's details and rooms with just a click. Customers can search and filter rooms by hotel, date, and number of guests directly from the website homepage.
Odoo Hotel POS Extension The hotel management module functionality can be extended to add your room service order to the final bill at checkout. Odoo Hotel POS Restaurant Extension permits you to create invoices of room service orders and add them to the final bill at hotel checkout.
Note: The module is compatible with POS on which you need to purchase the Odoo Hotel POS Restaurant Extension.

Detailed Features List

Improved and Dynamic Dashboard

  • Odoo Hotel Management Solution can manage hotel operations effortlessly from a single screen.
  • Access check-in and check-out requests, available/booked rooms, and room types.
  • You can view real-time updates for a more efficient workflow.

Multi-Hotel Management

  • View and manage all your hotels from the backend in one place.
  • Click on any hotel to manage its details and rooms easily.
  • Add new rooms using the "Add Room" option available inside each hotel.
  • Customers can search for rooms hotel-wise on the website homepage.
  • Filter rooms by hotel, check-in/check-out date, and number of members (adults/children).

Hotel And Its Detail Page

  • Display hotels in both grid and list view formats on the website.
  • Each hotel has its own dedicated detail page for better presentation containing images, about, policies, services and reviews.
  • A map is added to the hotel page to help users locate it easily.
  • Improves the browsing experience by offering hotel-specific details at a glance.

Enhanced Booking Management

  • You can book hotel rooms directly from Odoo backend.
  • Admin can view, modify, and cancel bookings when needed.
  • Syncs bookings between the website and the backend for accuracy.

Enable Advance Payment

  • Advance payment is a configurable setting that can be enabled or customized per hotel.
  • A specific pre-payment amount can be set from the hotel's form view in the backend.
  • The "Advance Payment" button appears on the booking screen when enabled.
  • Clicking the button opens the customer payment form and links it to the booking or sales order.
  • When an invoice is created, the advance amount is automatically adjusted in the total bill.

Housekeeping & Room Services Automation

  • Housekeeping tasks are assigned automatically after guest checkout.
  • In Odoo Hotel Management Solution, manual assignment is available for better control.
  • You can track service history and add charges to the final bill.

Advanced User Roles & Access Control

  • Assign roles like receptionist, housekeeper, or laundry staff to manage tasks efficiently.
  • Control staff access to ensure secure handling of hotel operations.
  • Set permission levels based on job roles, so everyone gets the right access.

Smart Invoicing & Commission Management

  • Create invoices automatically or manually when a guest checks out, making billing quick and easy.
  • Set agent commissions as a fixed amount or percentage, making it simple to manage.
  • You can add services like laundry or dining to the final bill for a smooth checkout.

Seamless Email Notifications

  • Send automatic emails for booking updates, like confirmations and cancellations, to keep everyone informed.
  • You can keep guests and staff updated in real time to prevent confusion.
  • Make communication easier, ensuring a smooth experience for both guests and staff.

Customer Rating

  • Customers can give star ratings and add comments through the mail link.
  • Admin can send a feedback mail to customers after they check out.
  • Admin can group and view ratings by hotel or booking etc.
  • Helps track customer satisfaction and improve services based on real feedback.

Advanced Reporting to support strategic decision-making

  • Get detailed reports on bookings, revenue, and occupancy.
  • Download dynamic reports to highlight key info and choose specific periods.
  • Easily share or save reports by exporting them to Excel.

Dynamic Services

  • Easily manage additional guest services like transportation, laundry, dining, and special requests.
  • Add dynamic services with their prices, and easily include them in the final bill.
  • Offer guests a variety of service options to create a customized experience.
