Odoo Hotel Booking Channel Manager

Odoo Hotel Booking Channel Manager acts as a bridge between your Odoo and popular OTA platforms like Booking.com, Expedia, Airbnb, Agoda, Google Hotels, etc. Whenever a booking is made on connected OTA platforms, the module syncs the booking details to Odoo and updates inventory to other OTA platforms in real time.

Why QloApps Channel Manager?

Qlo Channel Manager is an integration that connects QloApps PMS with popular OTA platforms. It enables real time synchronization of inventory, rates, restrictions, and bookings across all connected channels, ensuring accurate updates and preventing overbookings.

How does the Odoo Hotel booking channel manager work?

A Channel Manager helps you manage your hotel's room availability and prices across different online platforms. Our Odoo Hotel booking channel manager connects your Odoo hotel system to the QloApps channel manager, syncing your room availability instantly with online travel agencies (OTAs) through the QloApps channel manager. When a booking is made on any platform, it automatically synchronizes the booking to Odoo in real time, ensuring your hotel's inventory is also updated across all connected OTA platforms. This streamlines your hotel operations and prevents overbooking.
Note: This plugin provides integration with the OTA platforms through the QloApps channel manager(Webkul's own hotel booking platform).
So, to use this plugin, you will have to pay a 30 USD/month ( for each property with unlimited rooms) for the QloApps channel manager, which will integrate your Odoo with OTA platforms.

Why SaaS Based Pricing for QloApps Channel Manager?

We aim to provide fixed costs for most of our plugins, and 95% of our Odoo plugins follow this pricing model. However, when it comes to OTA platform integrations, the situation is different. Many OTAs do not provide direct APIs to hotels. Instead, they prefer to offer APIs to a single intermediary, the Channel Manager, who handles all their partner hotels. This simplifies the process of updating rates, availability, and reservations across multiple properties and standardizes communication between OTAs and hotels, reducing integration complexity.
For OTAs, working with channel managers also allows them to scale more efficiently. Instead of developing and maintaining thousands of direct API connections, OTAs can manage their relationships with a few channel managers.
So, to enable seamless integration with OTA platforms, we provide a SaaS-based pricing model for using the QloApps Channel Manager, which ensures ongoing support, scalability, and a smooth experience for all users.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the subscription plans for the QloApps Channel Manager?

QloApps Channel Manager offers two pricing options:

  1. Monthly Plan: $30 USD per month per property. For 6 or more properties, the cost reduces to $25 USD per month per property.
  2. Yearly Plan: $300 USD per year per property. If you have 6 or more properties, the yearly cost is $250 USD per property.

Is there a trial period available for the Odoo QloApps Channel Manager?

Yes, we offer a 10-day trial period during which you can connect QloApps Channel Manager with Odoo. However, during the trial, OTA platform connections are not available.

Is Odoo QloApps Channel Manager a SaaS-based solution?

Yes, Odoo QloApps Channel Manager is a SaaS-based solution, allowing hoteliers to manage bookings, inventory, and rates online.

Can I manage unlimited rooms with Odoo QloApps Channel Manager?

Yes, you can manage unlimited rooms with Odoo QloApps Channel Manager, making it suitable for hotels of all sizes.

How do I set up Odoo QloApps Channel Manager?

Setting up the Odoo QloApps Channel Manager is simple. Follow the detailed instructions in our blog here: QloApps Channel Manager Setup Guide.
