Hotel POS Restaurant Extension

Calculate all POS purchase bills in Odoo!

Need of Hotel POS Restaurant Extension

Ordering food while in a hotel or availing of room service is common. Customers enjoy their meals in their rooms, but the problem arises while billing because sometimes some bills are left unpaid inducing loss to the hotel.
So how do you calculate the correct bill without missing room service orders?
Odoo Hotel POS Restaurant Extension is created for this purpose. The module enables you to invoice all room service orders and maintain a separate list of POS orders and room charges. You can print the bill in a combined or isolated format. Not only this, it allows customers to clear their bills along with other Hotel bills conveniently.

Get the Most Out of the Hotel POS Restaurant Extension Module

This Odoo App is an extension of the Odoo Hotel Management Solution and is fully compatible with it.
By using both modules you can easily manage your restaurant and hotel bookings from the same single screen. You can bill and invoice every room service order and room charge in the final hotel bill.
Get Odoo Hotel Management Solution to manage your hotel booking and room services efficiently.

Quickly Pay Hotel POS Restaurant Bills!

The module club together all the POS and Hotel bills into a single combined or isolated printed statement, which you present to the customer to receive the payment. All the order details are printed on the bill so customers can verify them and make the payment.

Ordering Items by Odoo POS

The Odoo app adds a 'Add Room' button in the POS, allowing the POS user to select the room that placed the order and add items the customer ask. Once done, you can invoice the charges. They will be automatically added to the room booking bill in a separate section.

Detailed Features List

Sync Hotel Management with Odoo POS

  • The module integrates Odoo POS and Odoo Hotel Management Solution
  • It allows the generation of invoices for room service bills.

Add and select the room to provide service

  • You can choose the room in the POS with the 'Add Room' button.
  • After adding a room customer will be selected accordingly, and you can place the order.

Billing of the POS Order

  • The Hotel management POS order will be invoiced when a customer order it.
  • Customers can conveniently pay the bill at checkout along with other room bills.

Computing Bills at the Customer Check out

  • The Odoo admin can compute the customer room and POS bills at checkout.
  • You can compute an isolated and computed bill per the customer's or admin's preference.
